Folding Styles Available

Choose from our comprehensive range of professional folding options to create the perfect brochure for your needs.

TRI-FOLD

Tri-Fold

Perfect for product brochures and detailed information. Creates 6 panels for maximum content space.

Z-FOLD

Z-Fold

Accordion-style fold ideal for step-by-step guides and sequential information presentation.

BI-FOLD

Bi-Fold

Simple and elegant half-fold design. Great for event programs and simple product showcases.

GATE FOLD

Gate Fold

Premium fold style with dramatic reveal effect. Perfect for high-impact marketing materials.

ROLL FOLD

Roll Fold

Multiple panels that roll out in sequence. Excellent for detailed product catalogs and manuals.

FRENCH

French Fold

Double fold creating 8 panels. Ideal for wedding invitations and premium announcements.

PARALLEL

Parallel Fold

Horizontal folds creating elongated panels. Great for timelines and process explanations.

FLAT

No Fold (Flat)

Single flat sheet perfect for posters, flyers, and large format promotional materials.

170 GSM Art Paper

250 GSM Art Card

Best for: Luxury brochures, portfolios

300 GSM Art Card

Gloss Lamination

High-shine finish that enhances color vibrancy and provides excellent protection against wear and moisture. Perfect for vibrant images and photos.

Matte Lamination

Smooth, non-reflective finish that's fingerprint resistant and easy to write on. Ideal for text-heavy brochures and professional documents.

Uncoated

Natural paper texture without coating. Eco-friendly option that's easy to write on and perfect for educational materials and forms.
