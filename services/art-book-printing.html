Art Book Paper Options

Premium paper selection is crucial for art book reproduction. Our museum-quality papers ensure faithful color reproduction and archival longevity[41][50].

Coated Gloss Paper

High-shine finish that enhances color vibrancy and depth. Perfect for photography and fine art reproduction with exceptional image clarity.

Weight: 157-250 GSM
Finish: High Gloss

Coated Matte Paper

Elegant non-reflective finish with excellent print quality. Reduces glare while maintaining rich color reproduction and fine detail.

Finish: Matte Coated

Uncoated Paper
