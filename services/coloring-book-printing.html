Coloring Book Paper Options

The right paper choice is crucial for coloring books. Our uncoated papers prevent ink bleed-through and provide the perfect surface for crayons, colored pencils, and markers.

Uncoated Paper

Perfect for coloring with any medium. Our uncoated paper provides excellent ink absorption and prevents bleed-through to the next page.

Specifications

Material: Uncoated White
Weight: 70-100 GSM
Thickness: 0.097-0.118 mm
Finish: Matte Natural

Coated Gloss/Matte Paper

Premium coated paper for covers with enhanced color vibrancy and professional finish. Available in both gloss and matte options.

Specifications

Material: Art Paper
Weight: 250-350 GSM
Thickness: 0.225-0.410 mm
Finish: Gloss/Matte

Natural Shade Paper

Eco-friendly natural shade paper with a warm, cream tone that's easier on the eyes and perfect for extended coloring sessions.

Specifications

Material: Natural White
Weight: 80-100 GSM
Thickness: 0.105-0.118 mm
Finish: Natural Matte

Coloring Book Binding Options

Choose the perfect binding method for your coloring book based on page count, budget, and usability requirements.

Saddle Stitch Coloring Books

Cost-effective binding perfect for coloring books up to 64 pages. Allows books to lay flat for easy coloring.

Wire-O Binding Coloring Books

Premium binding with 360° page rotation. Perfect for adult coloring books and professional applications.

Hardcover Coloring Books

Luxury hardcover binding for premium coloring books, gifts, and collector editions.

Perforated Coloring Books

Pages with perforated edges for easy removal. Perfect for classroom use and sharing finished artwork.
