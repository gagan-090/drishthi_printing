Specifications & Guidelines

Everything you need to know about our standees and design requirements.

Available Sizes

  • 2ft × 5ft (Standard)
  • 3ft × 6ft (Large)
  • Custom sizes available

Materials

  • 180GSM Photomatt PP film
  • Premium vinyl options
  • Weather-resistant coating

Resolution Requirements

  • Minimum: 150 DPI
  • Recommended: 300 DPI
  • Large format: 72-150 DPI acceptable

File Formats

  • PDF (preferred)
  • AI, EPS, PSD
  • High-res JPEG, PNG

Production Timeline

  • Standard production: 2-3 business days
  • Rush orders: 24-48 hours (additional cost)
  • Custom sizes: 3-5 business days
  • Proof approval required before printing
