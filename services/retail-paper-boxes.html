Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for custom retail paper boxes?
Our minimum order quantity is 500 pieces for most standard designs. For complex die-cut shapes or special finishes, the minimum may be higher. Contact us for specific requirements.
How long does production take?
Standard production time is 7-10 business days after artwork approval. Rush orders can be accommodated with 3-5 day turnaround for an additional fee.
Do you provide design services?
Yes, our in-house design team can create custom packaging designs from scratch or work with your existing artwork. Design services are included for orders over $1,000.
What printing options are available?
We offer full-color digital printing, offset printing, Pantone color matching, foil stamping, embossing, debossing, UV coating, and specialty finishes like soft-touch or metallic effects.
